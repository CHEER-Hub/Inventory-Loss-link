Objective
---------

The objective of the FORTUNA-Link module is to combine the CHEER-Safe outputs (configuration lookup tables) and FORTUNA's building (-level) inventory given hurricane hazard scenarios (wind speed and flood depth at any grid system) to estimate losses at various levels.
